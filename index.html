Getting There

Venue

University of Fribourg
Fribourg, Switzerland

✈️ By Plane

  • Geneva Airport (GVA) — ~1 h 20 min by train (direct IC)
  • Zurich Airport (ZRH) — ~2 h by train (change at Bern)
  • Basel EuroAirport (BSL) — ~1 h 30 min by train
  • Bern Airport (BRN) — ~40 min by train

🚆 By Train

Fribourg has a main train station (Fribourg/Freiburg) with direct connections to Geneva (~1 h 20 min), Bern (~25 min), Lausanne (~50 min), and Zurich (~1 h 40 min). The university campus is a short bus or funicular ride from the station.

🚌 From the Station

  • Bus line 3 or 7 towards the Pérolles campus area
  • Funicular (Funi) connects lower town to upper town — free with a valid train ticket on the day of arrival

🚗 By Car

Fribourg is located on the A12 motorway between Lausanne and Bern. Paid parking is available near the campus. We encourage participants to travel by public transport where possible.
